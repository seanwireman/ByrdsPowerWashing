AT BYRD’S WE USE BOTH A SOFT WASHING SYSTEM AND PRESSURE WASHING SYSTEM

Soft Washing System – is a low pressure high flow cleaning system using less than 800 psi and up to 4 gpm of water flow. This system is the safest way to clean most buildings and houses as well as wood surfaces such as decks and porches. The low pressure causes no damage to window screens or painted surfaces as well as not forcing water behind siding or through weather stripping and seals.


Pressure Washing System – is a high pressure cleaning system using up to 4000 psi and 4 gpm. This system is used mostly on brick and concrete surfaces or badly stained areas.
